Artist Statement/summary:

Out in the land of the tobacco plant, comes a tall, hard-ridin', lean bloke. He goes by the name of Johnny Smoke.

Johnny Smoke is a 2D Sidescrolling platformer game that features the titular character exploring the desert in the search for Iron Lung. You control Johnny Smoke while shooting the Lungs that come your way.

This game was made in a collaborative effort between 4 classmates for their Game Design class. Joren was mainly responsible for bringing all art assets and creative elements together in Construct 2 which this game was built on.
